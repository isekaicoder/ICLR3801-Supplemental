Ensemble

For this experiment the explicit voxel grid model is used with different CLIP models, namely “RN50”, “RN50x4”, “ViT-B/32”, “ViT-B/16”, “ViT-L/14”, “ViT-B/32 + ViT-L/14”, “ViT-B/16 + ViT-L/14”. Training is done at image resolutions 224 * 224 and 15k iterations. The augmentations and KL loss are all turned on for these experiments.

The prompts from top to bottome are:

  1. “An elephant placing some leaves in its mouth with its trunk.”
  2. “The cat is sleeping comfortably on the chair.”
  3. “Bouquet of flowers sitting in a clear glass vase.”
  4. “matte painting of a bonsai tree; trending on artstation.”
  5. “Apples on tree ready to pick in garden area.”
  6. “A pizza that is covered in a lot of toppings.”
  7. “a old kitchen with no appliances inside of it.”
  8. “A stuffed animal sitting in the grass, with a book in front of it.”
